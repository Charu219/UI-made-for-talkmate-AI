Practice with Audio Correction

One of the biggest challenges in learning English is speaking naturally and clearly. Talkmate AI’s Audio Correction feature ensures that you not only say the right words, but also say them with the right fluency and pronunciation.

After completing your conversation, each corrected line is paired with an audio button. When you click it, Talkmate AI reads the corrected version aloud in a natural human-like voice. You can then compare it with your own attempt and notice differences in tone, speed, and accent.

This feature allows you to redo and repeat the same conversation until your pronunciation and fluency match the AI’s guidance. It acts like a pronunciation coach who never gets tired of correcting you.

For advanced learners, Talkmate AI can also act as a teacher that asks you questions on any topic you choose. You respond, the AI analyzes, and then plays back the corrected version. In Group Discussion mode, multiple voices are simulated so you practice fluency in a realistic setting. And for professionals, the HR Interview mode ensures you speak with clarity, confidence, and correct pronunciation.

With continuous listening, comparing, and repeating, you’ll gain the fluency and confidence needed to communicate effectively in English.
