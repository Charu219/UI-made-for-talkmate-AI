Get Detailed Feedback

Talkmate AI does more than just practice conversations—it becomes your personal English trainer. Once you start a dialogue, our AI listens carefully to your speech and analyzes it in real time. When you finish, simply click Stop and instantly receive a detailed breakdown.

You’ll see the difference between what you spoke and what you should have spoken. The platform highlights grammar errors, missed vocabulary, and pronunciation slips. Each corrected sentence comes with an audio playback button, so you can hear the correct version and compare it with your own. This helps you understand both grammatical accuracy and fluency in spoken English.

To make learning more effective, Talkmate AI also detects your fluency score, measuring pauses, filler words, and speed. If you are not satisfied with your attempt, click Redo and the same conversation will restart, allowing you to practice repeatedly until you gain confidence.

We’ve made the experience even more innovative. If you provide a topic, Talkmate AI will generate teacher-like questions and speak them aloud, guiding you through interactive learning. For group practice, it can simulate a group discussion, placing you in a virtual circle of participants. And if you’re preparing for a job, our HR interview trainer asks professional questions and gives constructive feedback to improve your performance.

With Talkmate AI, feedback is not just a correction—it’s a complete learning journey that adapts to your goals.
